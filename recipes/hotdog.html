Hot dog

Description

Looking for something to cure your rumbling stomach? Look no further than the boiled hotdog!

Ingredients

  • 1 store bought hot dog bun
  • frank's hot dog
  • duct tape

Steps

  1. Add water to a pot
  2. Bring the water to a boil
  3. Put the frank in the boiled water
  4. Boil hotdog for 7 minutes
  5. Turn off the heat and drain out the water
  6. Spread out ur hotdog buns
  7. Carefully lay the frank on the bun
  8. Garnish the glizzy with the duct tape
  9. Enjoy
