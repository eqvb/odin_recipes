Eric's Famous Cereal

Description

Have you ever had a midlife crisis in the ripe age of 53? I haven't yet, I'm 21. But I know just the recipe to cheer you up!

Introducing Eric's famous cereal! It's sweet, savoury, sour, and depending on where you come from, it could be spicy too!
